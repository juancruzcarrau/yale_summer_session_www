🎆 4th of July Fireworks 🎆

4th of July is one of USA's biggest celebrations, conmmemorating their Independence day in 1776 when seperating themselves from the British monarchy. Since then, each year people gather with their family or friends to rejoice in numerous ways, all the way from a simple gathering, going as far as to have massive parties. One thing is certain, they sure do love their special holiday, and proved as much in New Haven, Connecticut. Last friday, the city witnessed a marvelous firework show, which many people attended with their loved ones to hear good music, eat nice food, and have an oustounding time.
